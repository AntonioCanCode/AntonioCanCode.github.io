About Me

I love a good puzzle!

At first, I thought web development wasn't for me. Then I took charge of a Squarespace website for work, and that all changed. Once I had a really good handle on Squarespace I started feeling caged in. I knew there were ways I could add more and more functionality, but Squarespace wasn't offering it. That's when I started to learn how to code here and there. First, I fixed a nav bar, next a few buttons, and then I was hooked. Every time I set a goal, I had to figure out the code behind it. I found myself researching online for hours trying to make sense of the bits and pieces of code that i found. Then one day, I realized coding was for me because I loved figuring out the solutions to the various problems being in charge of a website presented. After taking a course at General Assembly, I discovered there was so much more I could do. Deciding to double-down, I took SkillCrush's Break into Tech Course, where I found new puzzles to solve and some amazing solutions to help me along the way.Now, I'm continuing to learn and constantly thinking about coding projects.

I'm Antonio and I love to code. Coding feels like a puzzle that I just have to solve. I often find myself losing track of time while I'm coding. I am a problem solver, an innovator, and a great teammate. In a team dynamic, I am the person who is always trying to find the best way to accomplish the task. I enjoy coming up with new ideas or expanding upon the ideas of my team mates. I don't mind having my ideas picked apart and creating something better together. I believe in uplifting my teammates because I don't just want to win, I want all of us to win. A win is better when it's shared. I work best in environments where I am challenged and where the pursuit of knowledge is encouraged.
